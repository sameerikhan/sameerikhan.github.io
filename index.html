Burger Recipe

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 1 person
Ingredients quantity
burger buns 1
beef patties 1
avacados 1
garlic 1
mayonnaise 1
tomato and red onion 1
cheese 1

steps

Step 1: Mix beef with onions and other ingredients.

Step 2: Fry patties on a grill.

Step 3:Lay buns down and toast.

Step 4: Put mayonnaise and ketchup on buns. Then add burger patty, tomatos and onions. Enjoy!

MacnCheese Recipe

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 1 person
Ingredients quantity
Milk 2 cups
butter 2 tablespoon
salt 1/2 teaspoon
Cheddar Cheese 10 OZ.
Macaroni 16 Oz.
flour 2 tablespoon.

steps

Step 1: preheat oven and melt butter. Add in the flour until its smooth.

Step 2:put in milk.

Step 3:put in Cheese.

Step 4: bake at 400 degrees for 20 minutes. Enjoy!
