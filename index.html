Burger Recipe

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 1 person
Ingredients quantity
burger buns 1
beef patties 1
avacados 1
garlic 1
mayonnaise 1
tomato and red onion 1
cheese 1

steps

Step 1: Mix beef with onions and other ingredients.

Step 2: Fry patties on a grill.

Step 3:Lay buns down and toast.

Step 4: Put mayonnaise and ketchup on buns. Then add burger patty, tomatos and onions. Enjoy!
